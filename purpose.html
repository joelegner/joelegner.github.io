Purpose

I want to contribute to helping the world by improving my own thinking. When I think clearly, I make better decisions. When I make better decisions, favorable outcomes become more likely. Doing things that increase the frequency of desirable outcomes seems like a good use of my time.

As the site develops, we will look at answering questions like this:

  1. What does clear thinking look like?
  2. What does unclear thinking look like?
  3. Can thinking about thinking do any good?
