What do I believe about human nature?

  1. Human behavior starts with ideas. Good ideas lead to the reduction of suffering. Bad ideas lead toward the increase of suffering.
  2. Human beings are made for cooperation. To defect against another human being is to reject the core value of human nature.
  3. All humans seek the good.
  4. When humans are seen to act wrongly, that is to increase suffering, it is because they have wrong ideas.

How do I want to be remembered?

The dead cannot suffer. Suffering is for the living. Let the living decide how best to remember the dead.
