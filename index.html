How do I state Joe's Prime Directive
Reduce suffering.
How do I define the word "suffering"?
Sense 1. The pain of living.
Sense 2. The abstraction of physical pain to include emotional and mental.
Sense 3. The feeling of not accepting things as they are.
Sense 4. The feeling I get when I have something I don't want or the similar feeling I get when I want something I don't have.
How do I describe my idea of suffering?
The feeling that goes with pain in body, heart, or mind.
What is my idea of understanding?
The mental equivalent of being close enough to a statue to be able to describe it well in your own words. The mental idea is the statue. Your understanding is your detailed description of it.
How do I imagine the idea of “idea”?
As a statue in a plaza which can be seen from many different, literal, points of view.
How do I imagine the idea of “point of view”?
One of the places someone is standing while looking at the statue we call “idea”.
How do I imagine the idea of the word “point” in the phrase, “she was making a point”?
A person standing at a particular point of view points their finger at a feature of the statue we call “idea” while they are talking to describe it. Making a point is visualized as pointing with a finger.
Why all this metaphor about a statue?
I have a vague hypothesis that this is the clearest, most direct way to understand concepts.
What is the key to all relationships?

What is the key to good judgment?

What is the most common cause of bad judgment?
Judging before one understands.
